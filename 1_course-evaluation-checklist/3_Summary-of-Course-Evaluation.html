Objective

To summarize information regarding the Course Evaluation Checklist

Summary

In this module we took a high level look at the Course Evaluation Checklist v2.0, including identifying 10 Foundational Items that could be used as a quick start guide for Canvas course improvement. A successful digital learning environment should use thoughtful design to ensure course materials are approachable by students of all backgrounds.

Items within the checklist correspond to four areas of focus: Course Information, Course Content, Assessment of Student Learning, and Course Accessibility. Future modules will explore each of these focus areas, providing insight, examples, and opportunities to engage with other educators on their implementation.
