Summary

In this module we took a high level look at the Course Evaluation Checklist v2.0, including identifying 10 Foundational Items that could be used as a quick start guide for Canvas course improvement. A successful digital learning environment should use thoughtful design to ensure course information, content, assessments, and accessibility are approachable by students of all backgrounds.

Future modules will explore each of these areas of focus in detail.
