Course Card

1.6 | Course Card provides a visual representation of the subject by adding an image in Course Settings. UDL2.5

While some students may have a single course at a time, many are juggling mutliple courses, each trying to convince them of its importance in their life. Including a custom course card can reinforce course goals and create visual appeal tailored to learners. At the very least it will make your course look cooler than others.

What Does It Look Like?

Branding!

Create "branding" for your course. Use a course banner and card to create a consistent design style with common font elements and colors. Unlock your inner designer!
