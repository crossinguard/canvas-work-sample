Contact Information

1.5 | Contact information is provided, including communication preferences and response time expectations, with an optional biography or picture. UDL8.3

Detailed instructor information and communication preferences fosters an open, transparent learning environment where students clearly understand and can adapt to expectations. Streamlining out-of-class or asynchronous interactions benefits all parties while maintaining a health boundary between work and life.

What Does It Look Like?

Teacher Bio

Share a photo and lower the barrier to communication by including relevant and interesting tidbits about your journey. You know you are a human and not a knowledge robot, but do they?

Manage Expectations

Clearly outline availability expecations and typical response times to help students understand norms and plan accordingly. Students won't expect 24/7 access if boundaries are well defined and in place.

Provide Options

One method of communication rarely covers all needs. Investigate available tools of your institution and built-in Canvas tools to create a pallete of contact options to present to students. Don't forget to check that inbox!
